I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science and Engineering at University at Buffalo, State University of New York. Prior to that, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in EECS at Harvard from 2010-13. I received my M.S. and Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Southern California in 2010.

My research interests are in sensing and software systems challenges in mobile systems and robotics.
