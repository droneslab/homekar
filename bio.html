Short Biography of Karthik Dantu

Karthik Dantu is an Assistant Professor in Computer Science and Engineering at University at Buffalo, State University of New York. Prior to that he was a Postdoctoral Fellow in EECS at Harvard from 2010-13. He received his M.S. and Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Southern California in 2010.
His research interests are in sensing and software systems challenges in mobile systems and robotics.
